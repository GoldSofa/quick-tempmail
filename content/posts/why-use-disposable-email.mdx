### Why Use Disposable Temporary Email?

1. **Prevent Spam Harassment:** Many websites send massive amounts of marketing emails, newsletters, or promotional messages after user registration, or even sell email data to third parties. Temporary emails act as a buffer, keeping your real inbox clean.

2. **Protect Privacy & Anonymity:** Temporary emails do not require registration and are not associated with your real name or personal information. After the email usage period ends, the system deletes your personal details, address, and IP address, achieving complete anonymity.

3. **Enhance Security:**

    - **Avoid Data Breach Risks:** If a small or untrusted website you registered with gets hacked, what you leak is only an expired temporary address, not threatening your real email and its associated sensitive accounts.

4. **Barrier-Free Usage:** Email addresses are usually automatically generated without extracting passwords or manually selecting hostnames, obtained instantly.
